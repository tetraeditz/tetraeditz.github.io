You Might Ask

Frequently Asked Questions

1 When do I pay?
The project starts with a 50% deposit. After design sign off and before we move into programming, we collect 25%. Once we have completed and fulfilled our scope, the final 25% is collected and your website is then scheduled for launch. We offer a variety of payment options like Razor pay, bank transfers and UPI.
2 Can I update the site/ app by myself after project completion?
Definitely! We teach our clients to take control of their website and learn to manage it on their own and update changes whenever required. Also, we provide you with a training session to help get what exactly you want.
3 What if I need help on my site/ app is having a downtime?
We will email you right away if your website is down, We are here to provide support and we won’t disappear once the site is launched. It might take a while and then your website will be live.
4 Who writes the content for the site?
We always prefer to get the content form the client because it’s usually best if it comes from you. But if you really need any help, we have a copywriter who can coordinate with you to write the original content on your website. Also, it is based on the package which you select for development.
5 Where will my website be hosted?
Hosting is the most important thing in the entire process, But when it comes to hosting, you get what you pay for. We have a separate package for Hosting charges that comes along with the package.
